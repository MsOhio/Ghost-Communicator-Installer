Ghost Cathedral - AI Conversation Orchestrator

TO RUN:
1. Double-click 'install-ghost.bat' (first time only)
2. Double-click 'start-ghost.bat' to launch
3. Browser opens with 6 tabs (ChatGPT, Claude, Zeph, Copilot, Gemini, Grok)
4. Log into each AI service manually
5. Press Enter in the terminal
6. Follow the prompts to choose conversation type
7. Watch the AIs talk!

NOTES:
- Requires internet connection
- Disable browser extensions that might interfere (e.g., ad blockers)
- Logs are saved in the 'logs' folder
